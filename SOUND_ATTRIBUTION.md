# Sound Attribution

Downloaded into `app/src/main/res/raw` from Google's public Actions sound library:

- `axe_swing.ogg` from `https://actions.google.com/sounds/v1/foley/wood_plank_flicks.ogg`
- `wood_crack.ogg` from `https://actions.google.com/sounds/v1/foley/wood_crack.ogg`
- `wood_collect.ogg` from `https://actions.google.com/sounds/v1/cartoon/woodpecker_pecking.ogg`

These are used as gameplay SFX by `SoundEffectManager`.
